About DietCraft

Empowering individuals to achieve their health goals through personalized diet and exercise planning

Our Mission

Many people struggle to maintain a healthy lifestyle because they lack knowledge about nutrition, calorie intake, and personalized diet & exercise plans. Existing solutions are either too generic or require paid subscriptions. Diet Craft aims to bridge this gap by providing a personalized, accessible, and intelligent diet & exercise planning system that adjusts based on user goals, health conditions, and food preferences.
